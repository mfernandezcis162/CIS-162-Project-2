Freighter Jobs

As a part of your "free" transportation, there are various jobs that one must perform in order to earn their "free" ride

Coal Trimmer

- Also known as a stoker, this position requries you to shovel coal into the furnace in order to generate steam to power the ship
this job can be dangerous due to inhaling toxic fumes and risk of burning. This position is only necessary if the ship is steam powered.

Fire Fighter

- As a firefighter you are the first line of defense if in the unfornuate case, a fire breaks out

Sewage Treatment

- Although this job does not sound fun, it is important. treating raw sewage in order to keep the ship running in a sanitary manner is important
Custodial Crew
- As a custodian you will be tasked to clean all areas of the ship and perform basic maintenance
